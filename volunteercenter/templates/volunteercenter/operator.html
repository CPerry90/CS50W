Instructions

Here, you can view your assigned orders and available orders.


Accepted Orders are Green.

Fulfilled Orders are Grey.

New Orders are Purple.

Processing orders are Yellow.

Overdue orders are outlined Red.


When viewing new or processing orders, you can choose to accept the order, which will add it to your assigned orders.


When viewing your assigned orders, you can choose to complete the order, if the order is complete.


On any order view, you can download a PDF of the order to print.
